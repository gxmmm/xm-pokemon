# arena-bg.png

Optional 1:1 pixel-art stadium background, 1000x700 (20:14).
Drawn BEHIND the procedural oval floor + grid as the stands/sky. When absent, a
procedural stadium (concentric seat rings, biome-tinted) is drawn.
Drop at sprites/battle/arena-bg.png to replace the procedural stands background.
The oval floor, grid lines, and arena wall are always procedural on top.
